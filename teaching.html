Teacher Assistant

I am the head lab teacher assistant for EE16A. Before this position, I have lab assisted for CS61A, EE16A, and then promoted to lab teacher assistant. Responsibilities include organizing a BOM for the students' kits, lecturing about lab material, maintaing a clean and organized lab, debugging student's labs, and managing the grading infrastructure. Lab Development is also one of my responsibilities. I work closely with graduate students and professors that have taught or will teach 16A to help guide the direction of the course. Aside from that, I also write midterm and final problems, proctoring exams, and training TAs and lab assistants weekly for upcoming labs.
